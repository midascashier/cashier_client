CASH-OUT POLICY

www.pokerking.com's commitment to making sure that your funds are safe is absolute. To assure the security of all money held in trust by us, we store our members' account funds in a separate bank account. We are in discussions with a number of organizations on how to make this process transparent to ensure that our customer's funds are safe with us. And that no matter the circumstance our customer's funds will be protected and returned if necessary. We are committed to providing our customers an acceptable solution by the end of March. As soon as we have a definite solution we will publish it on our website.

It is our goal to provide the best service possible and provide you with expedient cash-outs. On this page you will be able to find cash-out expectations for each method we offer.

When you cash-out, your transaction will be completed via the payment method(s) with which you have deposited money into your account when the option is available.

For practical purposes and to ensure that you receive the most value for money (i.e. do not lose a large sum due to applicable fees), we will process your cash-out transactions via the appropriate payment method(s) as follows:

Any cash-out transactions made via your www.pokerking.com account will be conducted using the oldest (i.e. first used), active deposit method linked to your account with credit card transactions taking precedence. In this case, we take 'active' to mean that the method is still linked to your PokerKing.com account.

For more information about our cash-out policy please contact PokerKing Support at support@www.pokerking.com with your inquiries

Cash Out Limits & Player to Player Transfer Policy

Cash Out & Player to Player Transfer Policy

The minimum amount which can be cashed out by players for any online payment method is $5.

www.pokerking.com absorbs processing cost of all deposits and one withdrawal per calendar month. The second and any consecutive withdrawal during the same calendar month, will be subject to a withdrawal fee of 5%. In an effort to help us keep cost down so we can provide you with these services, the earliest you may request or process a withdrawal, is 48 hours after your last deposit. Please note that we reserve the right to pass the processing cost entirely to you should we determine that you are abusing this privilege.

Players can use Visa as a cashout option for all Visa deposits and winnings up to $500. Any extra winnings should be cashed out via a different payment method.


DEPOSITING PLAYERS

There are no other restrictions that apply to depositing players. All winnings generated after the initial deposit date* will have no requirements and can be cashed out and/or transferred to another player.
* Active sessions started prior to deposit time are not to be taken into account. Deposit time to be considered once all current active sessions have ended.
Definition of depositing player for cash-out:
To be considered as a depositing player you must fund your account with real money, by making a real money deposit in our cashier.

NON DEPOSITING PLAYERS

From time to time PokerKing will offer promotions to attract new players. These promotions are intended for players who would like to check our products and services before they decide to deposit and play. We understand that these promotions will also attract players who do not have any intention to deposit. It is for those players that we established the following rules and requirements.

Non Depositing Players who have won real money

Winnings from Real money tournaments & cash tables have no requirements and can be cashed out and/or transferred. Funds won in Freeroll/Received via Player to Player Transfer will carry the 3.3 SP for every $1 rule.

Wire transfer and bank transfer withdrawal options are not available to non depositing players. Requests for these payment methods will be declined and the withdrawal amount will be returned to the account balance.

Freeroll winnings for non-depositing players

All non depositing players who have won money from freeroll(s) must generate 3.3 status points for each 1 USD of free money received before requesting a withdrawal and/or player2player transfer. Any request made before satisfying this requirement will be declined and the withdrawal amount will be returned to the account balance.

Non-depositing winners must provide proof of identity in order to receive cash-out and/or to approve player to player transfers.

Free Money Bonus Coupons

All recipients of free money bonus coupons must generate 6.6 status points for each 1 USD of free money received before requesting a withdrawal and/or player2player transfer. Any request made before satisfying this requirement will be declined and the withdrawal amount will be returned to the account balance.

Player to Player Transfers

Maximum transfer amount from player to player is subject to Poker King management discretion.
Management reserves the right to reject cash-outs for transferred funds or impose wagering requirements.
Non-depositing players who are a recipient of player to player transfers may only cash-out amounts greater than the initial transfer. To clear the initial transfer player must earn 3.3 Status Points for each 1 USD received in order to successfully request a withdrawal or a Player to Player Transfer to another account. Any request made before satisfying this requirement will be declined and the withdrawal/P2PT amount will be returned to the account balance.

No previously earned Status Points will count towards releasing of a received P2PT.

Player may not transfer free money that has yet to be cleared to another player.

Requesting a Cash-out

You can request a cash-out at any time in the Cashier section of your www.pokerking.com account by selecting the 'Withdrawal' tab, entering the amount of money to withdraw from the funds in your account and clicking submit.

Please note: only real money funds can be withdrawn. Play money, Bonus money and Tournament money cannot be withdrawn or exchanged for a cash alternative.

Once you have submitted your cash-out request, you will be redirected to a page indicating the method used to process your request. You will need to thoroughly check all information on the screen to be certain that you agree with the chosen method and are aware of the steps which will be taken on your behalf. If you agree with the method displayed, clicking on 'confirm' will begin the transfer process.

PokerKing is unable to accept responsibility for all information displayed during the cash out process and cannot guarantee against any delay or error occurring during the transfer process.

If any of the information displayed during the transfer request is invalid or obsolete (i.e. an expired credit card), please cancel your request and contact our support department via email to support@www.pokerking.com with the correct information.
