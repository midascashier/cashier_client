WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW

DEVELOPMENT strives to fulfill all payout requests as quickly as possible. In order to ensure your prompt payout, please read the following:

  • All payout requests usually take from to 2 to 3 business days to be processed and delivered.

    The 2 to 3 business day time period will begin once the funds are deducted from your account.

  • You may request one (1) payout every seven consecutive days. This time period is calculated starting from the most recent day on which funds were withdrawn from your account.

  • Please note: No payout request will be accepted until 72 hours after your last deposit, to allow our accounting department time to examine your past account transactions for any discrepancies.

    For more information about payout requests, please contact DEVELOPMENT Customer Service by calling 1-800-000-0001 or by Live Online Chat.

HAVE YOU EVER DEPOSITED USING A CREDIT CARD?

DEVELOPMENT can no longer refund deposits made using credit cards nor fulfill a payout request by directly crediting your credit card account. In order to better protect you from identity theft and guard you against fraudulent use of your credit card, if you deposit by credit card you will be required to fill-out a Credit Card Authorization Form verifying the original charges and must submit a copy of your valid ID before any payout from your account will be processed. Please download and print a copy of the Credit Card Authorization Form , or request the forms from DEVELOPMENT Customer Service.

All requests deemed eligible for free payouts will be processed through Debit Card ONLY. All other payout requests will first be credited back to any E-Wallet method you may have used to make your deposit up to the amount you deposited using that method. Any remainder may be delivered using the payout method of your choosing, if available.

HOW TO MAKE A PAYOUT REQUEST?

General Payout Guidelines

To request a payout, please go online and access the DEVELOPMENT Cashier by logging into your DEVELOPMENT account and clicking on the WITHDRAW tab. Choose your preferred payout method and follow the steps.

You may also contact DEVELOPMENT Customer Service between Monday and Friday by calling 1-800-000-0001, emailing cs@bookmaker.eu or by Live Online Chat. But please note that there is an additional $10 charge for all payout requests that are NOT made online.

Current payout methods include: MoneyGram, Western Union, Paper Check, Wire Transfer, and Neteller. Please read below regarding each method.

1. Paper Checks

To request a payout by paper check, go online and access the DEVELOPMENT Cashier by logging into your DEVELOPMENT account and clicking on the WITHDRAW tab. Then simply follow the steps. You may also contact DEVELOPMENT Customer Service via phone or LIVE Chat and submit your request, BUT there will be an additional $10 fee.

The daily cut-off time is 12 Noon ET Monday through Friday in order to begin processing that same day.

Your paper check payout request may take up to 7 business days to be processed and delivered.
Only one (1) paper check payout request is allowed per week, the maximum payout is $2500 per check, and the processing fee is $50 per check.

2.Neteller

To request a payout by Neteller, go online and access the DEVELOPMENT Cashier by logging into your DEVELOPMENT account and clicking on the WITHDRAW tab. Then simply follow the steps. You may also contact DEVELOPMENT Customer Service via phone or LIVE Chat and submit your request, BUT there will be an additional $10 fee.

The daily cut-off time is 3pm ET Monday through Friday in order to begin processing that same day.

For payouts, the processing fee is $25.

3. Western Union & MoneyGram

Western Union payouts are currently limited to a maximum of $1,500, and MoneyGram payouts are currently limited to a maximum of $900. For more information about the processing fees associated with these payout methods, please contact DEVELOPMENT Customer Service.
